Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download ProveMeWrong from the App Store, open the app, and tap "Sign Up." You can register with your email address or use Sign in with Apple for a quick setup.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ProveMeWrong → Cancel Subscription. Your premium features will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Can I request a refund?
Since purchases are processed through the App Store, refund requests must be submitted to Apple directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can delete your account from within the app: go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Alternatively, email us at brandon@celestibless.com and we'll process your request within 30 days.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
What platforms do you support?
ProveMeWrong is currently available for iOS (iPhone and iPad). We're exploring support for additional platforms in the future.
